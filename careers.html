Careers at L'atelier Eden

Are you passionate about doing the ordinary extraordinarily well? Are you curious to learn more, to always improve on each service and constantly pursue perfection?

We offer talented and self-motivated individuals the chance to be part of an ambitious and loyal team entirely dedicated to exceptional food, wine and service.

If you think this is you, please fill out the form below with your CV and reasons for wanting to join the L'atelier Eden team.

Please note we can only consider applications from those eligible to live and work in the UK.

We are hiring for the following positions:

  • Commis Chef
  • Senior Chef De Partie
  • Junior Sous Chef

  • Host/Hostess
  • Commis Waiter/Waitress
  • Waiter/Waitress
  • Sommelier

  • Kitchen Porter
